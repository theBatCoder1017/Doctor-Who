The Doctor is from a species known as the Timelords.

Timelords are beings who can travel through time and space and created the Tardis.

Timelords have the ability to regenerate. When the body of a Timelord dies, it regenerates into a new body, which makes Timelords immortal. However, timelords can "turn off" their regeneration if they wanted to.

Timelords have multiple enemies. Their main enemies are the Daleks. You can see a picture of Daleks below.

A main goal in a Dalek's life is to destroy every thing that isn't a Dalek.

So far, there have been 12 doctors.

The current doctor is played by Peter Capaldi.

Every Doctor has a companion because he wants someone to bring along on his journey and he gets lonely.

In addition to the Tardis, another tool that the Doctor uses is a sonic screwdriver.

  • Lock Picking
  • Burning/Cutting any substance
  • Amplifying Sound Waves and the Power of an X-Ray
  • Disarming Weapons and Electronics
  • Acting as a Flashlight
  • Intercepting and Conducting Teleportation
  • Dye Eyeglass Lenses to Make Them Sunglasses
  • Acting as a Microphone
  • Detecting and Reviewing Symbols
  • Conducting Medical Scans
  • Remotely controlling the Tardis and other devices
  • Tracking alien life
  • Controlling the Properties of Atoms and Molecules on a Small Scale
  • Operating Computers
  • Providing Geo-Location
  • Get Cash From an ATM
  • Light Candles
  • Modifying Mobile Phones
  • Deactivating Camouflage
  • Destroying Weeping Angels
  • Disarming Robotics
  • Tightening and Loosening Screws
  • Scanning and Classifying Matter
