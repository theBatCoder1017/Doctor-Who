What is a Tardis?

The Tardis is the main form of transportation that the doctor uses.

The Qualities of a Tardis

  • The Tardis can travel through space and time, which means that it is capable of time travel
  • The interior of Tardis is larger than the exterior of a Tardis because the interior is exists in a different dimension than the exterior.
  • The Tardis has a mechanism called the "Chameleon Circuit" which allows it to blend in with it's surroudings once it lands.
